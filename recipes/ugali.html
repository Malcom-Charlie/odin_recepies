ugali

Description

Ugali is a very common lunch/dinner dish in most of our homes. Cooking it is easy and can be served with vegetables, meat, soup or even fish

    Prep: 0 mins
    Additional: 45 min
    Total: 45 mins
    Servings: 1
    Yield: 4 plates

Ingredients

    1 Cup flour
    2 Cups water
    Butter

Steps

    1. Boil water. This ensures the ugali will cook perfectly.
    2. Add flour into the boiling water. Let it cook for 10 mins.
    3. Use a wooden spoon to mix the flour and water into a slightly soft mold.
    4. Reduce heat. Keep mixing and turning the mold
    5. Repeat step 4 until the mold stuck to the Sufuria side starts to peel itself.
    6. Flip the Cooked mold onto a plate. Best served hot
